The main files(i.e - script,html.css files) are present in activity folder. 
Firstly run index.html file so that u can do the following:-
1- U can access your camera and microphone .
2- U can chnage filter with the help of available filter bar
3- U can record your videdo by clicking on Red(recording) Button. & U can stop by again clickingon red Button.
4- U can capture your picture with different different filter by clicking on White(capture) Button.
5- U can click gallery icon where you can see your clicked photo & recorded video. 
6- when you reach your gallery, you can donwload/delete photo and video by clicking delete and donwload Button.
